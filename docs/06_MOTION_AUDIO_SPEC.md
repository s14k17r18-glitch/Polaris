# 06_MOTION_AUDIO_SPEC.md

## 1. Motion Language
- **Style**: "Fluid Sci-Fi". Sharp starts with smooth dampening (Ease-Out-Expo).
- **FPS**: 60fps target.

## 2. Key Sequences

### 2.1 The Genesis Sequence (Persona Creation)
*Total Duration: 5.0s*
1.  **Phase 1: Skeleton (0.0s - 1.5s)**
    - Neon blue wireframe bones assemble from void.
    - Neural network nodes light up along the spine.
2.  **Phase 2: Body Forming (1.5s - 3.5s)**
    - Transparent "Glass" skin wraps the skeleton.
    - Digital fluid fills the glass shell.
3.  **Phase 3: Soul Ignition (3.5s - 5.0s)**
    - Burst of particles from the chest core.
    - Eyes glow (Ignition).

### 2.2 Routine Animations
- **System Boot**: Hexagonal grid scans across the screen. Logo pulses.
- **Resonance Join**: Persona's seat lights up, avatar materializes from particles (Heroic entrance).
- **Synapse Link**:
    - Trigger: Agreement.
    - Visual: A beam of light connects two Holo-Nodes.
    - Audio: High-pitch "Ping" sound.
- **Crystallization**:
    - Log text swirls into a center point.
    - Compresses into a glowing gem (Red/Blue).

## 3. Audio Design (SFX)
- **UI Interaction**: Glass "Clink" sounds (high frequency).
- **Ambient**: Deep drone (Space station hum).
- **Voice**: All voices processed with slight reverb to match "Large Hall" acoustics.
